Me

I'm Leander, a 20-year-old avid learner of Japanese on the road to humbly conquer the lowest language level that's out there: N5.
Since I often happen to use Romaji to Kana converters (and being the 7th semester software engineer, that I am) I had to create such a tool myself. Mainly because existing ones have such an unpleasant user interface, that's just not fun to use. So here we are. Nice to meet you!

Japanese

My journey into the language began in my final years of high school, when I taught myself Japanese from a couple of text books. Due to lack of time, I had to abandon the endeavor for a while until towards the end of university the opportunity arose to take even two Japanese courses at once. The whole project is dedicated to that outstanding teacher, who made us show up on Friday morning and love it.

Mission Statement

The purpose of this website is to provide a beautiful, easy-to-use and blazingly fast Romaji to Kana converter. It shall be available to everybody as a free tool and 100% secure through being an open source software. And I promise: there will never be annoying ads... just good user experience! Romaji2Kana aims to make your life just a bit easier, by doing what it does best: converting Romaji into Kana and the other way round.
